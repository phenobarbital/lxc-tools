LXC-tools Templates
===================

* Debian: wheezy, jessie, sid
* Ubuntu: LTS 12.04
* Canaima: roraima, auyantepui, karepakupai
* CentOS: 5.5, 6.0, 6.3, 6.4
* Fedora: < 15 (Fedora > 16 systemd are incompatible with Debian)
* Gentoo: 2013 stage3
